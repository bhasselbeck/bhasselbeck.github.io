EVENTS OF THE DAY

In order to ensure our wedding day runs smoothly, here is a detailed, wedding day timeline in order to keep the day organized, stress-free, and on time! Please use it to plan accordingly for you and/or your plus ones and family!

6-6:15 PM9/5/2021

ARRIVE AT THE LEGARE WARING HOUSE

Plentiful complimentary guest parking!

Shuttle transportation from the parking area is provided for guests. The venue does not encourage leaving cars in the parking area overnight, but if you need to leave a car overnight, it must be parked in the main visitor center parking lot. It can then be picked up the next day during park hours.

6:30 PM9/5/2021

CEREMONEY IN THE AVENUE OF THE OAKS

The Legare Waring House's avenue of oaks is an iconic ceremony location in the heart of the Lowcountry. The ceremony and reception will be at the same venue, and an air-conditioned, tented space will be provided alongside the canopy oak trees!

7-7:45 PM9/5/2021

COCKTAIL RECEPTION, LIGHT HORS D'OEUVRES

The Legare Waring House has the ability to accommodate up to 200 guests indoor/outdoor, but we anticipate around 120 people to attend the wedding. There should be plenty of space to roam. Restrooms will be inside the house.

7:45 PM9/5/2021

PERHAPS A TOAST OR TWO

Have a special toast or send out to the wedding couple?

8:00 PM9/5/2021

LET'S EAT, CASUAL BUFFET STYLE

All dinner accommodations will be provided buffet style where guests will have the opportunity to pick and build their own plate. There will be tables inside and outside!

9:00 PM9/5/2021

DANCE THE NIGHT AWAY

We will have a stage with a lighted live performance by "The Charleston Wedding Band"!

10:45 PM9/5/2021

SAFE TRAVELS

Safe travels home! Uber event vouchers/gift cards will be provided to each family or carpool to ensure a safe ride home!
